# Inclusive Voice-First Survey Platform

## Mission

Create the world's most accessible survey platform for blind and visually impaired users.

The platform must support:

* Total blindness
* Partial blindness
* Low vision
* Color blindness
* Cataracts
* Glaucoma
* Elderly users

The platform must be usable on:

* Desktop
* Mobile
* Tablet

---

## Core Principles

Accessibility First

Voice First

Minimal Cognitive Load

Privacy By Design

Multilingual Support

Recovery From Failure

---

## Modes

### Self Guided

Voice-first interaction.

No microphone button after permission.

Automatic listening.

Automatic speech detection.

Automatic confirmation.

---

### Assisted

Voice, keyboard, mouse and touch supported.

---

### Low Vision

Large text.

High contrast.

Reduced motion.

Zoom safe.

## Non Negotiable Rules

No microphone button in Self Guided Mode.

Every answer requires confirmation.

All pages keyboard accessible.

All pages screen-reader accessible.

All state transitions announced.

All progress auto-saved.
